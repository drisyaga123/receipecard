VEG PASTA

  • 200g pasta (penne, fusilli, or spaghetti)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  5. Stir in the red bell pepper and zucchini. Cook until vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add cherry tomatoes and tomato sauce. Stir well and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce and mix until well combined.
  8. Top with grated Parmesan and fresh basil, if desired. Serve hot.

    9. Preparation time: 30 minutes

    Serving size: 2-3
